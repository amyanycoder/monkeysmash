The universe is made of trillions of tiny coincidences. What if you never asked out that boy you met at the bookstore? What if your parents never met? What if a microplanet never collided with the Earth, and gave us the moon and the tides? Life is a symptom of coincidence. The world would not exist except for brilliant, furious chance. So take a chance and enter: See what beauties serendipity may create.
